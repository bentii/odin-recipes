Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is an italian recipe made by italian chefs in Italy.

It mainly made with cheese and meat but some heretics make it with eggplant.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Meat
  • 3 Tomatos
  • Salt

Cooking Process

  1. Take a bowl
  2. Place in the order, first the pasta
  3. Then the meat
  4. Last the cheese
  5. Repeat till the lasagna get high enought
  6. 30min in the oven with sliced tomatos
  7. It's done!
